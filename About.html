 WHO AM I?

This is no easy question to answer, but I'll give it my best shot. My name is Zachary Edmondson, and I am currently a sophomore multimedia journalism major at Bob Jones University. I am a child of God who likes to ponder why we are here, and what our purpose in life is. I enjoy reading, writing, walking, and traveling to faraway places. 


My life's goal is to become a publications editor, and to write novels from a Biblical perspective. I write articles for BJU’s newspaper, The Collegian, and creative nonfiction for Inkwell Literary Magazine. I am also interested in doing voice work for radio.
